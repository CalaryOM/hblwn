Who We Are

A Community Built on Dignity & Solidarity

The Homabay LBQ Network is a community-based organisation founded by and for Lesbian, Bisexual, and Queer women in Homabay County. We believe every woman deserves safety, healthcare, and the freedom to live authentically.

Our work spans health services, legal aid, economic empowerment, and community building — all rooted in the lived realities of LBQ women in our region.

What We Do

Our Programs

⚕️

Health & Wellness

Accessible healthcare services, mental health support, and sexual reproductive health rights education tailored for LBQ women.

⚖️

Legal Aid & Advocacy

Legal assistance, human rights education, and policy advocacy to protect LBQ women from discrimination and violence.

💼

Economic Empowerment

Skills training, microfinance support, and entrepreneurship programs to build financial independence among LBQ women.

📚

Education & Awareness

Community sensitization, school programs, and public education to foster understanding and acceptance of LBQ rights.

🛡️

Safety & Protection

Safe housing referrals, crisis intervention, and security support for LBQ women facing violence or displacement.

🤝

Community Building

Safe spaces, support groups, and social networks that strengthen solidarity and reduce isolation among LBQ women.
